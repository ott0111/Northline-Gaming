About Northline

Northline is a gaming lifestyle brand and creator agency built for the next generation of competitive gaming creators. Founded in 2026, Northline represents over 40 of the most driven creators across competitive gaming, with a combined monthly audience reach exceeding 120 million viewers across 18 countries.

The Northline platform operates across six pillars: Creator Agency (representation and career management), Community (a private network for creator mentorship and collaboration), Software (tools for analytics, scheduling, and career management), Apparel (performance and lifestyle gear), Events (live experiences and community gatherings), and Media (original shows and stories).

Key Facts

  • Founded: 2026
  • Headquarters: Global (remote-first)
  • Creator Roster: 40+ across FPS, Battle Royale, Racing Sim, Strategy, Variety, and Fighting Games
  • Combined Reach: 120M+ monthly viewers
  • Countries Represented: 18
  • Mission: Build the infrastructure competitive gaming creators need to sustain and scale their careers
