Privacy Policy

This page outlines how Northline handles personal information collected through the site and related experiences.

Northline collects information you provide directly through forms, newsletter signups, and creator applications. This may include your name, email address, platform details, follower counts, and your message or interest in Northline’s services.

We use this information to respond to inquiries, process applications, send relevant updates, and improve the experience of visitors and community members. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

What We Collect

Depending on the interaction, we may collect contact details, professional information, social links, and application content shared through forms on our site.

How We Use It

We use personal data to manage applications, communication, marketing updates, and service-related requests. We may also use aggregated or anonymized information for internal analysis and reporting.

Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as needed to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information, subject to applicable law. You may also unsubscribe from marketing emails through the opt-out links in those messages.

Contact

If you have questions or requests about this policy, please reach out to hello@northline.gg.
