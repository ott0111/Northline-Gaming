Our Story

Built from the need for better support around creators.

Northline began as a practical response to a gap in the gaming ecosystem: the people shaping the culture often had to navigate deals, growth, and opportunities without enough strategic support. We wanted to build a home that treated creative work as a long-term career, not just a moment of attention.

That vision led to an agency model first — representation, negotiation, and career planning. It also led to something larger: a brand that could grow with creators across community, software, apparel, events, and media.

Mission

Careers should be designed with the creator in mind.

We work at the intersection of opportunity and stewardship. Our role is to create the conditions for creators to thrive with clarity, stability, and a stronger network around them.
